SPROUT — PHASE 6 REAL-WORLD TEST CHECKLIST
(Adaptive, moisture-based watering. Watering is now a soil CHECK, not a
calendar command, and each plant learns its own dry-out rhythm from your
feedback. Test on your real phone/browser.)

BEFORE YOU START — one setup step, required:
  [ ] Run supabase/migration_005_adaptive_watering.sql in the Supabase SQL
      editor. Until you do, the Today screen will 500 (the code now reads a
      column that doesn't exist yet). It's idempotent — safe to run once.
  [ ] After running it: open the app's Today screen and confirm it loads at
      all (no error). If it errors, the migration didn't apply — re-run it.

WATER CARD — LOOK & COPY (Today screen, needs a plant with a water task due):
  [ ] A water reminder now says "Check soil", NOT "Water", with the plant name
      above it.
  [ ] Below it: a tip like "Feel the top 2" of soil — water only if it's dry."
  [ ] Tap "How do I check?" — a guide expands with three methods (finger,
      chopstick, lift-the-pot). Tap again — it collapses.
  [ ] Two buttons at the bottom: "Still moist" (outline) and "Watered" (solid,
      green, with a droplet). Non-water reminders (rotate/repot/harvest) still
      show the single round check button — confirm those are unchanged.

FEEDBACK LOOP — "STILL MOIST" (the core new behavior):
  [ ] On a due water card, tap "Still moist".
  [ ] The card disappears from Today (it's rescheduled, not marked watered).
  [ ] Open that plant's care sheet. Under Watering, the expected interval
      should have grown a little vs. the species baseline shown below it, and
      you should see a line noting it was still moist / learning to wait longer.
  [ ] Wait for it to come due again (or note the next-due is only a couple days
      out, not a full cycle). Tap "Still moist" a few more times over days —
      confirm the expected interval keeps stretching (it's learning this pot
      dries slowly), but does NOT grow without limit.

FEEDBACK LOOP — "WATERED":
  [ ] On a due water card, tap "Watered". Card leaves Today.
  [ ] Care sheet: the "last time it was still moist" note is gone; next water
      check is scheduled a full (learned) interval out.
  [ ] EARLY WATER: on the plant sheet, before the plant is due, tap
      "I watered it just now". Confirm it returns you to the same plant page
      and the expected interval TIGHTENS a bit (you watered early → Sprout
      learns this pot dries faster).

PLANT CARE SHEET — WATERING SECTION:
  [ ] Watering section leads with "don't water on a clock — check the soil".
  [ ] Shows "Sprout expects {plant} to get thirsty about every N days right
      now" — and N reflects the current season (not a stale summer number in
      winter).
  [ ] Shows the species guide line (~Xd spring/summer, ~Yd fall/winter).
  [ ] The how-to-check guide (finger / chopstick / lift) is always visible here.
  [ ] "I watered it just now" button is present and works.

EXISTING PLANTS (added before this update):
  [ ] Any plant you added earlier should have its water check pulled in to
      within ~2 days (so the learning loop starts soon), NOT still weeks out.
      Confirm an old plant shows a water check due soon on Today.
  [ ] A plant that was already overdue should stay at its earlier date, not get
      pushed later.

MORNING DIGEST (optional, if push/email set up):
  [ ] Trigger the morning cron (or wait for it). In the email, water items read
      "Check soil (water if dry)", not "Water".

LANGUAGES (optional):
