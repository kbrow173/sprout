SPROUT — PHASE 2 REAL-WORLD TEST CHECKLIST
(Add-by-Photo + Claude. Do these on your phone, ideally with real houseplants
nearby — the more varied the better.)

RUN LOCALLY:
  [ ] From the sprout folder: npm run dev
  [ ] Open the app on your phone (same wifi, use your computer's local IP)

HAPPY PATH — CONFIDENT ID:
  [ ] Tap the (+) Add button → "Take a photo"
  [ ] Point at an easy, distinctive plant (e.g. a Monstera or Snake Plant) and shoot
  [ ] A preview of your photo appears with "Taking a look…"
  [ ] Within a few seconds: a confirm card shows a common + scientific name
  [ ] Tap "Yes, that's it 🌱" — "Gathering care info…" appears briefly
  [ ] The usual questions appear (potted/soil/drainage/light/nickname/date)
  [ ] Submit — you land on a full care sheet, and the plant appears on Garden

UNCERTAIN ID:
  [ ] Photograph a less distinctive plant, or a leaf close-up with no context
  [ ] Instead of one confirm card, you should see "Not 100% sure — which one
      looks right?" with up to 3 tappable options
  [ ] Tap one — flow continues into the questions step like the happy path
  [ ] Confirm "None of these — search manually" link goes to the library search

NOT A PLANT:
  [ ] Take a photo of something that is clearly not a plant (your ceiling, your
      hand, a wall)
  [ ] Confirm you get a friendly error: "Couldn't find a plant in that photo" —
      not a fake plant name, not a crash
  [ ] "Try again" retakes; "Search manually" jumps to the library

CHOOSE FROM LIBRARY (photo picker, not camera):
  [ ] Tap "Choose from library" and pick an existing photo of a plant
  [ ] Confirm the same ID → confirm → questions flow works

RETAKE / CHANGE YOUR MIND:
  [ ] Start a photo ID, then before it finishes tap back / immediately pick a
      DIFFERENT photo — confirm the app doesn't get confused (no mismatched
      species+photo, no stuck spinner)
  [ ] From a confirm card, tap "Not quite — retake photo" — confirm you're back
      at the camera/library buttons, not stuck

SAVED PLANT HAS A PHOTO:
  [ ] After saving a plant via the camera flow, open its care sheet — it should
      look identical to a manually-added plant (illustration art, not your raw
      photo — that's intentional, the illustration is always what's shown)

EDGE CASES (try to break it):
  [ ] Airplane mode mid-flow (after tapping a photo) — confirm you get a clear
      error message, not a silent hang
  [ ] A very blurry/dark photo — confirm you still get SOME response (an
      uncertain ID or "couldn't find a plant"), not a crash
  [ ] Add the SAME plant twice via photo — confirm the second one is instant
      (cached care profile) rather than waiting again for Claude to "think"

REPORT BACK: anything that felt slow, confusing, or wrong — especially any case
where the wrong plant name ended up on the wrong photo, or the app got stuck on
a spinner.
