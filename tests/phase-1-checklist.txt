your Supabase project. Do these on your phone + desktop.)

  [ ] Open http://localhost:3006

ADD A PLANT (manual path — camera comes in Phase 2):

  [ ] Type "snake" in the search box — confirm it filters to Snake Plant / ZZ Plant
  [ ] Tap "Snake Plant" — confirm follow-up questions appear (Potted?, soil,
      drainage, light, nickname, date)

      yellow "We'll recommend a mix for you" box

  [ ] Type a nickname, e.g. "Spike"

  [ ] You should land on a full care sheet: watering days, light, soil, rotate/
      repot, propagation, pruning, toxicity, a green "Do" list, a coral "Don't" list

GARDEN GRID:
  [ ] Tap "Garden" in the bottom nav — your plant appears as an illustrated card
      with its nickname and species name
  [ ] Add a second, different plant (try a succulent like Jade Plant) — confirm
      the grid shows 2 cards side by side with DIFFERENT illustrations
  [ ] Header now reads "2 plants"

CARE SHEET DETAIL:
  [ ] Tap a card — confirm it opens that exact plant's care sheet (not the wrong one)
  [ ] Confirm sections you'd expect are there; for an herb (Basil/Mint) there
      should be an extra "Harvesting" section that other plants don't have

DELETE:
  [ ] On a plant's care sheet, tap "Remove from garden"
  [ ] Confirm a browser confirmation popup appears asking to confirm
  [ ] Confirm it — you should land back on Garden with that plant gone

EDGE CASES (try to break it):
  [ ] Search for something that doesn't exist ("xyzzy") — see the friendly
      "No matches" message, not a blank screen or crash
  [ ] Visit a made-up plant URL like /plant/does-not-exist — see a normal
      "page not found," not a crash or blank white screen
  [ ] With zero plants, Garden and Today both show the friendly empty-state
      illustration with an "Add a plant" button

REPORT BACK: anything that looks off (wrong illustration for a plant type,
text overflowing a card, a button that doesn't respond) and we'll fix it.
